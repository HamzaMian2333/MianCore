# Product Requirements Document

## Product Name

MianCore

## Product Vision

MianCore is a safety-first AI operations dashboard for small businesses. It helps business owners understand their sales, manage inventory, draft customer orders, and approve important AI-generated actions from one dashboard.

## Target Users

Initial target users:
- Bakeries
- Cafés
- Dessert shops
- Small food businesses
- Small retail stores

## Problem

Small business owners often struggle with:
- Knowing what to restock
- Knowing what to prepare for tomorrow
- Understanding sales trends
- Missing phone orders when they are busy
- Managing inventory manually
- Making decisions without clean data

## Solution

MianCore uses AI agents and data analysis to:
- Analyze sales data
- Recommend restock and prep quantities
- Generate daily business summaries
- Draft phone orders
- Show all recommendations in a dashboard
- Require owner approval for important actions

## MVP Features

### Must Have

1. User can upload a sales CSV.
2. Dashboard shows revenue, product sales, and trends.
3. Inventory agent recommends what to restock or reduce.
4. Daily business agent generates a plain-English summary.
5. Mock phone orders appear as drafts.
6. Owner can approve, reject, or edit draft actions.

### Should Have

1. Recommendation history.
2. Agent activity log.
3. Basic business profile settings.
4. Sample bakery dataset.
5. Exportable daily report.

### Later Features

1. Real Twilio phone calls.
2. Shopify/Square integration.
3. Payment links.
4. SMS/email confirmations.
5. Supplier order drafts.
6. Multi-location support.

## Success Metrics

For the MVP, success means:
- A business owner can upload sales data and understand what happened.
- The dashboard generates useful recommendations.
- The AI explains recommendations clearly.
- The owner feels in control.
- No risky action happens without approval.

## Safety Principle

The AI should never act like a fully autonomous business owner. It should act like an assistant.

The AI can:
- Analyze
- Recommend
- Draft
- Summarize
- Flag risks

The owner must approve:
- Orders
- Payments
- Customer messages
- Inventory changes
- Pricing changes
- Supplier purchases
